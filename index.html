Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you visit after I book?

We offer same-day or next-day visits across major cities in KSA. Urgent cases are prioritized within 24 hours.

Are your treatments safe for children and pets?

Yes. We use IPM methods and approved products with strict application controls. We’ll brief you on any temporary re-entry times.

Do you provide documentation for audits?

Absolutely—SOPs, device maps, service reports, CAPA actions, and trending suitable for ISO, HACCP, and brand audits.

What pests do you cover?

Cockroaches, ants, rodents, termites, flies/mosquitoes, stored-product insects, and more—including industrial fumigation.

Which areas do you serve?

Nationwide coverage with local teams in Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam/Khobar, and additional cities by appointment.
