Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Still unsure? Contact us.

How fast can you visit?
We aim for same-day response in major cities and within 24–48 hours in surrounding areas. Use “Request Visit” to book the earliest slot.
Are treatments safe for children and pets?
Yes. We use targeted IPM methods and label-approved materials with clear re-entry times. We’ll brief you on ventilation and any prep needed.
Do you provide documentation for audits?
Absolutely—SOPs, device maps, trending logs, CAPA plans, and visit reports aligned to Saudi regulator language and NPMA best practice.
What if pests come back?
We stand behind our work. If activity returns within the service window, we’ll re-treat affected areas at no extra charge.
Which areas do you cover?
We cover the KSA with priority response in Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, Khobar, and Jubail. Contact us for schedules in other regions.
