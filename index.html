FAQ

How fast can you attend urgent issues?

Same-day where possible in major cities. We prioritize health & safety risks and food-safety facilities.

Are your methods safe for children and pets?

Yes. We follow IPM principles, minimize chemical load, and select products with appropriate safety profiles.

Do you provide audit-ready documentation?

Yes—SOPs, registers, monitoring logs, and CAPA plans aligned with regulator language.

Which pollutants do you measure on-site?

PM, CO, NO₂, O₃, SO₂, H₂S, noise levels, plus met data. Custom targets available per project.

Can you manage EIA and permit applications?

End-to-end: study scoping, assessments, submissions, and follow-ups with authorities.
